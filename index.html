I'm an aspiring cybersecurity enthusiast and perpetual learner who is fascinated by new technologies and the puzzles they present. I enjoy digging into systems, finding weak spots, and turning those discoveries into stronger, more resilient designs—always with curiosity and a healthy dose of caution. I like to balance hands‑on tinkering with reading and experimenting, building small projects to test my ideas. Above all,

I prefer working alone — not out of snobbery, just because I do my best thinking with a soldering iron in one hand and a quiet playlist in the other. I get into a flow where a stubborn bug, a messy breadboard, and a blinking terminal are all the company I need; conversations tend to interrupt the good part. That said, I’m not a hermit: I’ll reach out when a problem needs two heads, and I genuinely enjoy sharing the victory dance afterward.
